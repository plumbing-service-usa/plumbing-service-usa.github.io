Top Plumbing Repair Tips for Fort Worth Homeowners


Tip 1: Protect Your Plumbing System from the Heat Fort Worth summers can be scorching hot, but did you know that high temperatures can also cause your plumbing system to expand and contract? This can lead to cracks and leaks in your pipes, which can be costly to repair. To prevent this, consider installing a whole-house fan or investing in a thermal expansion tank. Tip 2: Be Mindful of What You Flush In Texas, it's not uncommon for homeowners to experience clogged drains due to flushing the wrong items. To avoid this, never flush anything other than toilet paper and human waste down your drains. This will help prevent clogs and keep your plumbing system running smoothly. Tip 3: Insulate Your Water Lines Freezing temperatures can cause water lines to burst, leading to costly repairs and potential water damage. To prevent this, consider insulating your water lines or wrapping them with heat tape. This will help keep your water lines from freezing and bursting. Tip 4: Check Your Water Meter Regularly In Fort Worth, it's not uncommon for homeowners to experience leaks due to old or damaged water lines. To catch leaks early, check your water meter regularly for any changes in usage. If you notice a sudden spike in usage, it may be a sign of a leak, which you can then have our plumbers fix.
