Top Plumbing Repair Tips for Austin Homeowners


**Tip 1: Be Mindful of Tree Roots in Your Drains** In Austin, tree roots can be a major contributor to clogged drains. This is because the city's numerous tree-lined streets provide a perfect environment for roots to grow and spread. To avoid this problem, be sure to regularly clean your drains and consider using a drain screen to catch debris before it enters your pipes. **Tip 2: Don't Forget to Insulate Your Pipes** As a homeowner in Austin, you're likely no stranger to the city's unpredictable weather. To prevent your pipes from freezing and bursting, make sure to insulate them thoroughly. This is especially important for pipes in unheated areas such as the garage or basement. **Tip 3: Know Your Water Line** In Texas, it's not uncommon for water lines to freeze and burst during the winter months. To avoid this problem, make sure to know the location of your water line and take steps to protect it from the cold. This may involve wrapping it in insulation or using a frost-proof spigot. **Tip 4: Schedule Regular Sewer Inspections** Finally, be sure to schedule regular sewer inspections to catch any potential problems before they become major issues. This is especially important in Austin, where the city's aging sewer system can be prone to clogs and backups. By staying on top of your sewer maintenance, you can avoid costly repairs and keep your plumbing system running smoothly.
