Common Plumbing Problems in Downtown, Las Vegas

Our Downtown technicians see these issues most frequently — here's what to watch for in your home

💦 Running Toilets

A toilet that runs constantly can waste up to 200 gallons of water per day. Our technicians identify the exact faulty component — flapper, fill valve, or float — and repair it on the spot.

🔩 Leaking Faucets & Fixtures

Even a small drip from a faucet wastes thousands of gallons annually. We repair or replace all faucet types, including ball, cartridge, ceramic disc, and compression valves.

⚙️ Sewer Line Problems

Tree roots, ground shifting, and aging pipes cause sewer line blockages and collapses. We use video inspection to diagnose the exact problem before recommending repair or relining.

🏗️ Water Pressure Issues

Both low and high water pressure indicate underlying plumbing problems. Low pressure may signal a leak or mineral buildup; high pressure stresses pipes and fixtures. We diagnose and fix both.
